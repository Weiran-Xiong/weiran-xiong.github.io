## About Me
Hi! I am Weiran Xiong, an M.Phil. student in Mathematics at the [Research Institute of Intelligent Complex Systems](https://iics.fudan.edu.cn/_s599/ywb/main.psp), [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/en/), from August 2024. I am delighted to study and conduct my research under the supervision of Prof. [Yunzhang Li](https://faculty.fudan.edu.cn/li_yunzhang/zh_CN/index/665374/list/index.htm) with the [Division of Financial Mathematics and Control Sciences](https://math.fudan.edu.cn/mathen/wivisionwofwwinancialwwathematicswandwwontrolwwciences/list.htm) at the [School of Mathematical Sciences](https://math.fudan.edu.cn/mathen/main.htm). Before that, I obtained a dual B.S. degree in Mathematics and Economics from [Sichuan University](https://en.scu.edu.cn/), where I developed a strong interest in interdisciplinary research, particularly at the intersection of Mathematics, Data Science, and Finance.

Below, you can learn more about my academic journey, research interests, and selected publications. If you are interested in any aspect of me, feel free to reach out for chats, discussions, and collaborations!
